Candle Care Guide

Get the most from your luxury candles with our expert care tips

Premium Materials

Our candles are crafted from 100% natural soy wax, sourced from sustainable American farms. Soy wax burns cleaner with no toxic releases, is biodegradable, and has a longer burn time than paraffin.

Signature Scents

We use premium fragrance oils infused with essential oils for our signature scents. Popular collections include "Amber & Sandalwood," "Vanilla Bean & Cedarwood," "Sea Salt & Orchid," and our seasonal "Pumpkin Spice & Clove."

Cotton Wicks

We use lead-free cotton wicks that are specially braided for a clean, even burn. Our wicks are carefully selected based on jar size and wax type to ensure optimal scent throw and minimal soot.

01

First Burn

Allow your candle to burn until the entire top layer of wax has melted, usually 2-3 hours. This prevents tunneling and ensures even burning for the life of your candle.

02

Trim the Wick

Before each burn, trim your wick to 1/4 inch. This prevents excessive smoke, extends burn time, and maintains a clean, even burn.

03

Avoid Drafts

Keep your candle away from drafts, vents, or air currents. These can cause uneven burning, sooting, or excessive wax consumption.

04

Burn Time

Don't burn your candle for more than 4 hours at a time. Allow it to cool before relighting to maintain the integrity of the vessel and the fragrance.
